# Privacy Policy – RiskBadge

RiskBadge does not collect, transmit, or sell any personal data.

## Data handling
- All analysis is performed locally in your browser
- No external servers are contacted
- No analytics, tracking, or telemetry

## Permissions
- `management`: used to read installed extensions
- `storage`: used to store local snapshots and alerts
- `alarms`: used for daily silent scans

These permissions are required solely for the extension's core functionality.
